JUDGING

Photos will be judged on originality, technical excellence, composition, overall impact, artistic merit, cleverness and other criteria the judges may deem appropriate.

Judging will be conducted by a Blackstorm employees and a guest judges as may be selected by Blackstorm at its sole discretion (the “Panel”). The Panel will evaluate all valid entries and pick the following winners in the following categories:

  1. Grand Prize Winner: You’ve nailed it. Your images rise above the rest and embody the joy of Everwing in a stunning way.
  2. Runner-up: You nailed it too!
  3. Honorable Mentions/Other: One or more other winners may be selected for beautiful, creative or noteworthy submissions and may be announced at later date.
